Hybrid / 6 months

Reverse Diabetes, Naturally

A deep hybrid program for people with type 2 diabetes, pre-diabetes or insulin resistance who need structure beyond random diet advice. It brings food rhythm, yoga therapy, movement, sleep, stress regulation, tracking and education into one disciplined system.

01

Stabilise

We start by calming the day: meal timing, hydration, walking, sleep cues, glucose observations and a simple practice rhythm that can survive real life.

02

Rebuild

Yoga therapy, breathwork, food discipline, stress regulation and digestion support are layered carefully so the body starts responding without overwhelm.

03

Personalise

Reports, medicines, cravings, work hours, family food, festivals and travel are used to refine the plan instead of breaking the plan.

04

Sustain

The final phase focuses on independence: knowing what to track, when to adjust, how to prevent relapse and how to continue safely.

Food

Diet becomes a rhythm, not punishment.

Meals are structured around timing, sequence, fibre, protein, cravings and cultural food realities so the person can eat with clarity instead of fear.

Practice

Yoga therapy supports glucose response.

Movement, breath and relaxation are matched to stiffness, energy, weight, age, stress and current capacity, then repeated until they become stable.

Tracking

Progress is measured without obsession.

HbA1c, fasting sugar, post-meal sugar, waist, sleep, cravings and energy become feedback signals for smarter decisions.
