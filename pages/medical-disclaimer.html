Medical disclaimer

Lifestyle care is not a substitute for medical treatment.

Nirog Bhumi supports natural metabolic healing, but diabetes care must remain medically responsible.

Read this before changing anything.

Nirog Bhumi does not diagnose, treat, cure or prevent disease. Guidance is educational and lifestyle-based. If you take diabetes medicine or insulin, do not reduce or stop it without your doctor. Blood sugar improvement can require supervised medication review.

Results vary

Remission depends on duration of diabetes, pancreatic function, medication history, weight, sleep, stress, adherence and other conditions.

Emergency care

Severe weakness, confusion, very high or low sugar, chest pain or breathing difficulty require immediate medical attention.

Doctor coordination

Our role is to help create the lifestyle environment for improvement while encouraging safe monitoring and responsible medical coordination.
