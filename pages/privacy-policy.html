Privacy

Your health details deserve care.

We collect only the information needed to respond, guide, process orders and support your healing journey.

01

What we collect

Name, email, phone, diabetes concern, HbA1c, medicines, routine and support needs may be collected through forms or consultation requests.

02

How we use it

Information is used for consultation, program interest, product orders, support communication and improving the quality of guidance.

03

Health sensitivity

Health information should be accessed only by authorised team members and used only for care, support and service delivery.

04

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of your information where legally and operationally possible by contacting the Nirog Bhumi team.
