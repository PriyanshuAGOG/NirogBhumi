Privacy

Your health details deserve care.

We collect and use only the information needed to respond, provide services safely and support your Nirog Bhumi journey.

01

What we collect

Basic contact details such as name, email and phone number, along with health information relevant to diabetes reversal: HbA1c and blood sugar readings, current medicines, diabetes history, lifestyle routine, food habits, sleep, stress, activity levels, support needs and information shared through forms, consultations or program requests.

02

How we use it

We use information to schedule and conduct consultations, assess program interest, process product orders, provide follow-up support, communicate important updates, personalise guidance and improve the quality, safety and relevance of our services.

03

Health Information Sensitivity

Health information is accessed only by authorised team members and used only for care, support and service delivery.

04

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of your information where legally and operationally possible by contacting us at priyanshu@nirogbhumi.com.
