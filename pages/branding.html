Logo rules

  • Do not stretch, crop, recolour or add shadows.
  • Do not place the logo inside a busy image.
  • Use the transparent PNG on white, paper or deep green surfaces.
02

Visual style

  • Use spacious layouts, thin borders and quiet surfaces.
  • Avoid loud gradients, heavy shadows and overly decorative shapes.
  • Let imagery feel real, natural and practice-led.
03

Voice

  • Clear, warm, responsible and educational.
  • Never promise a guaranteed cure.
  • Use diabetes reversal language with medical caution.

Typography

Editorial warmth with clinical clarity.

Use a serif voice for important headings and a clean sans-serif for body text, controls and structured information.

HeadingReverse Diabetes, Naturally

Georgia / editorial serif, regular weight, generous line height.

Cards

Cards use white or paper backgrounds, 8px radius for newer premium surfaces, thin borders and restrained spacing.

Photography

Prefer real yoga, food rhythm, consultation and natural health imagery. Avoid generic hospital stock or exaggerated wellness visuals.
