What we study

HbA1c, fasting and post-meal sugar, medicines, insulin, weight, waist, cravings, sleep, stress, digestion, food timing and daily constraints.

What you receive

A practical direction for food rhythm, yoga therapy, walking, breathwork, program readiness and safety conversations with your doctor.

What we do not do

We do not ask you to stop medication on your own. Lifestyle change must stay responsible, especially with insulin or hypoglycaemia risk.
